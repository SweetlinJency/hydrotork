Intersial valves

About Intersial valves

Interstitial valves are a type of linear motion valves used in piping systems. These valves allow, control, regulate, stop, or throttle the flow of fluids by moving their closure member in a straight line.

Product Offerings

  • Interstitial valves regulate the flow of fluid in one direction, allowing it to pass through while preventing reverse flow.
  • They ensure that fluid flows only in the intended direction, preventing damage to equipment and maintaining system integrity.
  • By preventing backflow, interstitial valves contribute to the safety of hydraulic and pneumatic systems, reducing the risk of accidents and malfunctions.

Why Intersial valves

Interstitial valves, found in hydraulic and pneumatic systems, are critical for preventing backflow and maintaining system integrity. Regular inspection and maintenance are essential to ensure their continued reliability and performance.
