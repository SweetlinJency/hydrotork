Fittings

About Fittings

Fittings in hydraulic systems serve as connectors between different components, facilitating fluid transfer and system assembly. These fittings come in various types, including adapters, couplings, elbows, tees, and unions.

Product Offerings

  • Fittings are used to connect various hydraulic components like hoses, pipes, valves, and cylinders together.
  • They provide a tight seal to prevent fluid leakage at connection points, ensuring efficient operation of the system.
  • Fittings allow for directional changes in fluid flow, accommodating the layout and design requirements of the hydraulic system.

Why Fittings

Fittings are essential components in hydraulic systems, facilitating fluid transfer and system assembly. They come in various types, such as adapters, couplings, elbows, tees, and unions.
